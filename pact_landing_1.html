How it works

Disarmingly simple.
Remarkably effective.

Social accountability is the most powerful behaviour-change mechanism known. Pact puts it in your pocket — without the feed, the likes, or the noise.

01

Build your circle

Invite the people who actually know you — friends, family, colleagues. Maximum 150. Mutual only. No strangers, no followers, no performance.

02

Make one commitment

Each evening, choose one thing you'll do tomorrow across four pillars: Move, Eat, Connect, or Grow. Your circle is notified. That's the accountability.

03

They hold you to it

Your people validate your completion. You validate theirs. Social reciprocity does what willpower alone never could. Then you close the app and live your life.

Four pillars

One commitment,
across all of life.

M

Move

Physical activity, exercise, outdoor time. A run, a gym session, a long walk. Your body, your pace, your commitment.

E

Eat

Nutrition, cooking, mindful eating. Meal prep, hydration, one good food decision. Not perfection — one step better.

C

Connect

Social interactions, community, human contact. Call someone. Meet someone. Show up. The world is lonelier than it should be.

G

Grow

Learning, creativity, personal development. A chapter read, a skill practiced, a new thing tried. Progress over perfection.

What is the 150-person limit about? +
Dunbar's Number — the maximum number of meaningful relationships a human being can maintain — is 150. Every other social app ignores this. Pact is built around it. No followers. No strangers. Just the people who actually know you.

Pact is a Swiss SA, built with privacy-by-design architecture. Your personal health and habit data is never shared with or sold to anyone — including organisations using our B2B product. Switzerland has the strongest data protection reputation in the world, and we intend to keep it that way.
